What is the area of a sector of a circle calculator?
The area of a sector of a circle is the portion of the circle enclosed by two radii and the corresponding arc.
How to calculate area of a sector of a circle calculator?
To calculate area of a sector of a circle calculator you should use the following formula:
A = (Φ / 360°) * (π * r^2)
where:
r = radius of the circle
Φ = angle of the sector
